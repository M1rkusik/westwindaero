About Us

WESTWIND Airlines is a virtual airline built on the principles of realism, quality, and development. We bring together pilots who strive for professional growth and exciting flights on modern airliners. Our route network covers Kazakhstan and international destinations, with a fleet of modern passenger and cargo aircraft. We offer a flexible career system, participation in KAZAKHSTAN VATRUS events, and a friendly community of like-minded aviation enthusiasts.
